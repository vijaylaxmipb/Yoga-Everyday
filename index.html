Why yoga everyday with us

Yoga Asanas

Yoga brings the body and mind together and is built on three main elements movement, breathing and meditation. Yoga has many physical and mental health benefits including improved posture, flexibility, strength, balance and body awareness.The practice of yoga asanas develops strength and flexibility, while soothing your nerves and calming your mind.

Breathing Exerises

Breathing is a core element of yoga practice. Yogic breathing, or pranayama, refers to conscious and controlled breathing that matches the rhythm of yoga postures.Yogic breathing, defined as a manipulation of breath movement, has been shown to positively affect immune function, autonomic nervous system imbalances, and psychological or stress-related disorders.
